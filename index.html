About Me!

My fascination with technology began at a young age, exploring the various computers and devices available today. In high school, I discovered a passion for coding. I started by learning the basics of computer programming through interactive tutorials, focusing initially on JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. My interest grew as I took computer science courses and completed an internship. Solving problems through code felt incredibly satisfying, and creating programs became a form of artistic expression for me.

Currently, I am working on new projects while learning how to use tools including Kubernetes for personal growth and practical application, collaborating with individuals and groups. While I have a preference for developing applications, I am also adept at creating websites that leverage my skills effectively. This involves mastering algorithms and frameworks that facilitate seamless user interactions. Additionally, I am actively engaging with the tech community, building relationships in a supportive and enjoyable manner.

I am pursuing a bachelor's degree in computer science at Florida International University. My commitment to self-improvement and continuous effort drives me to add value in every project I undertake.

In the future, I aspire to become a software engineer or developer at a reputable company. After earning my degree, my goal is to contribute to solving global challenges on a large scale, whether by advancing robotics, augmenting an existing tool, or creating something engaging and innovative like AI. As I gain more experience, I hope to specialize in a particular area of software development. Ultimately, I aim to make meaningful contributions to the social aspects of technology as well.
